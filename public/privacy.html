01 — Overview

Our commitment to your privacy

TawfiqTech ("we," "us," or "our") is a web design and AI automation agency based in Metro Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. We are committed to protecting the personal information of our clients, leads, and website visitors in accordance with Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL) and the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

02 — What We Collect

Information we collect

We may collect the following types of personal information when you contact us, complete a lead form, or use our services:

  • Contact details — full name, email address, and phone number
  • Business information — business name, type of industry, and location
  • Communication history — messages, emails, and form submissions you send us
  • Usage data — pages visited on our website, referral source, and device type

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial data, government IDs, or health information.

03 — How We Use It

How we use your information

  • To respond to inquiries and follow up on lead form submissions
  • To provide web design, AI automation, and related services to clients
  • To send service updates, proposals, and invoices to active clients
  • To send occasional marketing communications — only with your explicit consent
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors find us

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

04 — CASL Compliance

Marketing & CASL

We comply fully with Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). This means:

  • We only send commercial electronic messages with your express or implied consent
  • Every marketing message includes a clear and easy way to unsubscribe
  • We honour all unsubscribe requests within 10 business days
  • We keep records of how and when consent was obtained

If you submitted a lead form through one of our Facebook or Instagram ads, your consent to receive follow-up communications was obtained at the time of submission. You may withdraw that consent at any time by contacting us.

05 — Sharing

Who we share your data with

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We may share limited information with trusted service providers including:

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — for ad campaign management and lead forms
  • Google — for analytics and calendar integrations
  • Netlify / Supabase — for website hosting and database services
  • Twilio — for SMS and voice communication automations

06 — Retention

How long we keep your data

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Lead and client information is typically retained for up to 3 years. You may request deletion of your information at any time.

07 — Your Rights

Your rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

08 — Contact

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please reach out. We will respond to all privacy-related requests within 10 business days.
